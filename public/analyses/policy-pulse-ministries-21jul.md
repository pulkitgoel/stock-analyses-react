## 🏛️ SEBI Operationalises Promoter Share Freeze for Buybacks — Jul 21, 2026

SEBI on July 21 issued the **operational framework** for freezing promoter and promoter group holdings at the ISIN level during buybacks, directing depositories to implement by **August 1, 2026**.

### What Changed
- SEBI amended buyback regulations on July 1, 2026 (notification)
- Now operationalised: depositories must freeze promoter holdings from the date the board/shareholders approve a buyback until the offer closes
- Applies to both tender offer and the **revived open market buyback route** (reintroduced by SEBI, effective August 1)
- Promoters can still tender shares in tender offer buybacks and invoke pre-existing encumbrances
- Listed companies, exchanges, depositories, merchant bankers, RTAs must ensure compliance
