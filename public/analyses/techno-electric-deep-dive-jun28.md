## 2. Business Overview

Techno Electric & Engineering Company Ltd (TEECL), incorporated in 1963, is a leading power infrastructure EPC company with over 6 decades of experience. It provides Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC), asset ownership, and O&M services across the power value chain.

**Three Business Verticals:**

### 1. EPC — Core (~99% of FY25 Revenue)
- **Transmission & Substations (65% of order book):** EHV substations up to 765 kV — a high-complexity segment with limited competitors
- **Smart Metering (AMI):** 2.24 million meters under implementation under DBFOOT model across MP, Jharkhand, Tripura, J&K
- **FGD (Flue Gas Desulphurization):** Thermal power plant emission control systems
- **Key clients:** PGCIL, Adani Energy Solutions, Sterlite Power, RVPN, RECPDCL

### 2. Asset Ownership (TBCB Transmission)
- Two ISTS projects under TBCB framework (Gogamukh, Bokajan)
- Co-development with IndiGrid (Ishanagar + Dhule projects)
- Annuity revenue model — provides long-term recurring income

### 3. Data Centers (High-Growth Vertical)
- First facility (Gurgaon EDC) — **fully sold out**
- Cloud services commenced from Gurgaon facility
- Partnership with RailTel to deploy **102 edge data centers** across 23 states
- Targeting **250 MW hyperscale and edge data center capacity by 2030**
- 20-year revenue-sharing model with RailTel
- **Data center EBITDA margins target: 50-60%** vs 13-15% EPC margins

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Asset-light EPC model** — minimal fixed assets on books, high fixed-asset turnover
- **Premium margins** — 14% EBITDA margins vs industry peers at 7-9%
- **Selective bidding** — only bids complex EHV projects with assured funding
- **Near zero debt** — D/E of 0.02, almost debt-free
- **Strong order book** — ₹9,600 Cr unexecuted (2.5x FY27E revenue)
