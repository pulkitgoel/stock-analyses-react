## 2. Business Overview

Shakti Pumps (India) Ltd is a **Madhya Pradesh-based manufacturer of energy-efficient stainless steel submersible pumps, motors, controllers, and solar pumping systems**. Founded in 1995, the company has grown from a conventional pump manufacturer to a **leading player in India's government-driven solar pump ecosystem** (PM KUSUM scheme).

### Core Verticals

| Domain | Key Products |
|--------|-------------|
| **Solar Pumps** | AC/DC solar submersible pumps, solar surface pumps, controllers — primary growth driver via PM KUSUM & state schemes |
| **Agricultural Pumps** | SS submersible motors & pumps, openwell submersibles, centrifugal monoblock sets |
| **Industrial Pumps** | Effluent pumps, sewage pumps, chemical process pumps, fire-fighting systems |
| **Domestic Pumps** | Pressure boosters, borewell pumps, drainage pumps, home water systems |
| **Motors & Controllers** | ISI-marked motors, variable frequency drives, IoT-enabled pump controllers, SCADA |

### Business Model

- **B2G (Government):** ~65-70% of revenue from state government solar pump tenders (Maharashtra, Karnataka, UP, MP, Rajasthan, Gujarat)
- **B2B (OEM/Institutional):** Supply to irrigation depts, water supply boards, agricultural universities
- **B2C (Retail/Dealer):** Branded retail pump business through 2,500+ dealer network across India
- **Export:** Kenya ₹287 Cr order, expanding into Africa and Middle East

### Competitive Moat

- **Market leader in PM KUSUM solar pumps** — one of the largest beneficiaries of India's solar agri-pump programme
- **Vertically integrated** — in-house motor winding, controller manufacturing, testing lab, and upcoming DCR solar module plant
- **BIS/ISI-certified product range** — quality certification essential for govt tenders
- **Pan-India service network** — 500+ service centers for post-installation support (key differentiator for govt contracts)
- **Track record of executing large turnkey orders** — demonstrated capability with ₹650 Cr+ single orders

> *"We are transitioning from a pure pump manufacturer to an integrated renewable water solutions company with solar module backward integration."* — Management Commentary

## 16. Final Verdict

### **CAUTION — Margin Compression Story in Progress**

Shakti Pumps presents a **genuinely mixed picture**. On one hand, it enjoys a dominant position in India's solar pump ecosystem, a strong order pipeline (₹353 Cr MSEDCL order as recently as 4 Jul 2026), improving working capital management, and a P/E of 28.4x that is **well below the industry average of 45.2x**.

On the other hand, the **operating metrics are deteriorating alarmingly**:

1. **📉 Margin Collapse** — OPM has fallen from 25% (Q4FY25) to 10% (Q4FY26). If this persists, FY27 PAT could drop to ₹150-180 Cr, making the current P/E of 28x a **forward P/E of 40x+** on depressed earnings.

2. **📉 PAT Decline Trajectory** — PAT down -37% for FY26, -65% in Q4 alone. Three straight quarters of sequential PAT decline. The trend is not the company's friend.

3. **📉 Revenue Growth Deceleration** — From +84% (FY25) to +7% (FY26). While partly driven by working capital discipline, it signals that the low-hanging fruit of the PM KUSUM boom may be behind.

4. **🟡 Cheap P/E is Tempting — But Earnings Are Falling** — The stock trades at 28x, significantly below industry. But a stock that looks cheap on falling earnings is a classic value trap. Investors need to assess whether FY26 represents a temporary normalization or a structural margin reset.
