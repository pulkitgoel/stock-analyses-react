## 2. Business Overview

Kaynes Technology India Ltd is a leading **end-to-end IoT-enabled integrated electronics manufacturing services (IEMS)** company headquartered in Mysore. Founded in 2008, it provides conceptual design, process engineering, integrated manufacturing, and life-cycle support to major OEMs across multiple verticals.

### Core Verticals (Revenue Mix FY26)

| Vertical | Revenue Share |
|----------|-------------|
| **Automotive** | 55% |
| **Industrial** | 25% |
| **IoT / IT** | 10% |
| **Aerospace & Defence** | 6% |
| **Railways** | 2% |
| **Medical** | 2% |

### Strategic Growth Pillars

| Initiative | Status | Investment | Revenue Target |
|------------|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **OSAT (Sanand, Gujarat)** | Unit 1 pilot active, Unit 2 ready by Jul'26 | ₹3,307 Cr (50% central, 20% state, 30% company) | ₹570 Cr by FY27, ₹4,000 Cr by FY30 |
| **HDI PCB** | Unit 1 operational by Jul'26 | Part of ₹14 Cr total PCB capex | — |
| **Chamarajanagar Expansion** | Phase-I ready, 350K sq ft | ₹300 Cr est. | — |
| **Hyderabad Facility** | 70K SFT, SMT + injection molding | — | — |

### Competitive Moat

- **22 advanced manufacturing & design facilities** across India
- **13 global accreditations** — most for any ESDM company in India
- **200+ member R&D & engineering team**
- **500+ customers in 30+ countries** including marquee MNCs
- **India's first commercial Multi-chip module** from Sanand OSAT
- **Backward integration**: EMS → OSAT → HDI PCB — creating **full vertical integration** in electronics supply chain
- **Partnerships**: AOI Electronics (Japan tech partner), Mitsui & Co (supply chain), Alpha Omega (first paying OSAT customer)

> *"We are fundamentally redefining our position in the ESDM ecosystem. The strategic integration of our core EMS expertise with advanced OSAT and PCB manufacturing capabilities creates an unparalleled competitive moat."* — Management, May 2026 PPT
