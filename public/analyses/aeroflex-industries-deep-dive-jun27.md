## Business Overview

Aeroflex Industries (part of SAT Industries Ltd, 61.23% holding) manufactures stainless steel corrugated flexible hoses and hose assemblies — niche metallic flexible flow solutions for controlled flow of liquids, gases, and other media across industries.

**Key Segments:**

1. **Value-Added Products (52% of FY26 sales)** — Assemblies, fittings, bellows, and skid assemblies. Higher margin, growing faster.
2. **Standard Hoses (remaining)** — Stainless steel corrugated hoses and braids, end connectors, cam-lock couplings.
3. **Liquid Cooling Skid Assemblies (5% of FY26, scaling fast)** — Newest segment for data center and AI infrastructure thermal management. Capacity expanded to 6,000 skids/pa, targeting 15,000 by Q2 FY27.

**Key Stats:**
- Founded: 1993 (subsidiary of SAT Industries)
- 2,938 product SKUs
- Export-driven business (69% revenue from exports in FY26, down from 74% — domestic share increasing)
- Installed hose capacity: 17.5 million meters per annum
- Debt-free balance sheet (D/E 0.02)
