# Policy Pulse — Ministries — 07 Jul 2026

**Date:** July 7, 2026

## Overview

Major regulatory and procurement activity across Indian ministries this week: Defence Acquisition Council approved ₹52,000 crore in capital acquisitions, SEBI tightened buyback norms, India-EU FTA accelerated, and export promotion measures introduced.

---

## 🏛️ Ministries & Regulators — Indian Stock Impact

### Headline 1: DAC Approves ₹52,000 Crore Defence Procurement (Jul 3)

The Defence Acquisition Council, chaired by Rajnath Singh, granted Acceptance of Necessity (AoN) for proposals worth ~₹52,000 crore covering:
- **Indian Army:** Akash Tarang (anti-UAV EW system), MPATGM, MRSAM, V-SHORADS, Active Protection Systems for tanks, Jet-Based Kamikaze Drone Systems
- **Indian Navy:** Maritime Interdiction and Gunfire Support System (MIGM), Naval Shipboard Unmanned Aerial System (NSUAS), Land-Based Testing Facility for electric propulsion
- **Indian Air Force:** Fixed-Wing High Altitude Pseudo Satellites (FW-HAPS)

### Headline 3: SEBI Tightens Buyback Framework — Caps Stock Exchange Route at 15% (Effective Aug 1)

SEBI notified buyback amendments on Jul 1, effective Aug 1, 2026:
- Caps stock exchange buybacks at less than 15% of paid-up capital + free reserves
- Restricts companies from another buyback within statutory period
- Merchant banker appointment now optional
- Shares held by promoters frozen at ISIN level during buyback period
