# Policy Pulse — Modi — 03 Jul 2026

## India-Japan 16th Annual Summit: Historic Defence Pact, ¥10 Trillion Investment Target

Prime Minister Narendra Modi hosted Japanese PM Sanae Takaichi on July 1-3, 2026 for the 16th India-Japan Annual Summit in New Delhi. The summit delivered the **first-ever India-Japan defence co-development agreement**, a ¥10 trillion (≈$68 billion) investment target over 10 years, and 129 MoUs across AI, semiconductors, defence, energy, and critical minerals.
