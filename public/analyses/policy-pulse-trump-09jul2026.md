## 🇺🇸 Section 301 Forced Labour Hearings — India Defends on Day 3

Day 3 of the **USTR Section 301 forced labour hearings** (Jul 9, 2026). India formally argued before the USTR panel that the proposed **12.5% tariff** is inconsistent and should be resolved through **India-US Bilateral Trade Agreement (BTA) negotiations**, not unilateral Section 301 tariffs. India pointed out inconsistencies including exemptions for 1,600 products and reduced tariff rates for US-origin textile inputs. Poornima Shenoy (FICCI) and Suchita Sonalika (CII) also opposed the tariffs.

The proposed tariffs would replace the temporary 10% Section 122 tariff expiring **July 24, 2026**. A decision is expected after the hearings conclude.
