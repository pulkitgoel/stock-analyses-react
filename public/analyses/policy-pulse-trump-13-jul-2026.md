## 🇺🇸 Trump Signs Aircraft Trade Order — Section 232 Tariffs on Imported Commercial Aircraft, Jet Engines, Aviation Parts

President Donald Trump signed a new **executive order** adjusting tariff treatment of imported commercial aircraft, jet engines, and critical aviation components, following a **Section 232 national security review**. The order announced this week aims to rebuild America's aerospace industrial base, reduce dependence on foreign suppliers, and strengthen domestic production.

**Key details:**
- The Section 232 review concluded a strong domestic aerospace industry is essential to economic competitiveness and military readiness
- The order adjusts tariffs on imported commercial aircraft, jet engines, and aviation parts to encourage domestic production
- Builds on Trump's broader manufacturing agenda: steel, aluminum, copper, semiconductors, pharma tariffs
- Manufacturing employment has grown, major companies announced new domestic investments
- Critics warn tariffs could increase costs for manufacturers and airlines
