## 🏛️ DPIIT Amends Footwear Quality Control Orders — Strengthens Domestic Manufacturing

The **Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT)** announced amendments to two Footwear Quality Control Orders (notified Jun 12, 2026, published Jul 9, 2026) with two key changes:

1. **🇮🇳 Legacy Stock Clearance Extended to Jul 31, 2027** — One-year extension beyond the original Jul 31, 2026 deadline. Provides manufacturers, distributors, and retailers additional time for orderly liquidation of existing stock. Reduces compliance burden and trade disruption.

2. **🇮🇳 R&D Sample Import Exemption** — Manufacturers may now import up to **4,500 pairs of footwear annually** for R&D purposes. Samples must be marked "NOT FOR SALE" and disposed of as scrap. Enables evaluation of product designs, vendor presentations, and replication within India.

**Alignment:** Zero Defect, Zero Effect (ZED) manufacturing and Make in India objectives.

## 🏛️ Army Fast-Tracks ₹1,500 Cr Attack Drone Procurement — Tata, Nibe Win

The **Indian Army** has moved ahead with the **fast-track procurement of 850 one-way attack drones (loitering munitions)** worth approximately ₹1,500 crore, with supplies expected within 6 months of contract signing.

**Key details:**
- **Tata Advanced Systems Limited (TASL)** — Lowest bidder (L-1), to supply **64%** of the order (~₹960 crore)
- **Nibe Defence** — Second lowest bidder (L-2), to supply **36%** (~₹540 crore)
- **Range:** 100km+ loitering munitions
- **Features:** Resistant to jamming and spoofing; lessons from Operation Sindoor incorporated
- **Purpose:** Target enemy artillery positions deep across the border
- **Future pipeline:** Army plans to procure ~1 lakh drones; tender for **36 jet-based drones (~₹1,500 crore)** expected soon
- **Only 3 qualified:** Tata Advanced Systems, Nibe Limited (Vayuastra/Skystriker), A-Vision cleared technical evaluation
