## 2. Business Overview

Insolation Energy Ltd, incorporated in 2015 and headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a solar PV module manufacturer operating under the "INA" brand. The company is **the largest manufacturer of solar panels, batteries, and inverters in North India** and ranks among the **top 10 PV manufacturers in India**.

### Business Verticals

| Vertical | Description | FY26 Est. Rev | FY28 Target |
|----------|-------------|:------------:|:-----------:|
| **Solar Module Manufacturing** | Polycrystalline & Mono PERC / N-Type TOPCon modules | ₹2,800 Cr | ₹7,000 Cr |
| **EPC Services** | Integrated solar EPC (KUSUM, IPPs, rooftops) | ₹400 Cr | ₹1,100 Cr |
| **IPP (Independent Power Producer)** | Power generation from owned solar assets | ₹100 Cr | ₹470 Cr |
| **O&M Services** | Operations & Maintenance | — | ₹30 Cr |
| **Total** | | **~₹3,300 Cr** | **~₹8,600 Cr** |

### Capacity Expansion Roadmap

| Facility | Location | Capacity | Status |
|----------|----------|:--------:|--------|
| INA-1 & INA-2 | Jaipur | 950 MW | Operational |
| **INA-3** | Sawarda, Jaipur | **3 GW** | Operational (commissioned Aug 2025, 2-mth delay due to rains) |
| **INA-4** | Narmadapuram, MP | 3 GW Module + 4.5 GW Cell + 18,000 MT Al Frame | Under development (₹1,500 Cr capex, 75% IREDA financed) |
| **Cumulative Target by FY28** | | **7 GW Module + 4.5 GW Cell** | |

### Key Milestones
- **2015:** Company incorporated
- **2022:** Listed on BSE SME Exchange (IPO ₹38, listed at ₹76 — 100% listing gain)
- **2024:** Raised ₹395 Cr via preferential issue
