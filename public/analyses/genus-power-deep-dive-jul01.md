## 2. Business Overview

Genus Power Infrastructures Ltd, part of the Kailash Group (incorporated 1992), is India's leading smart metering company. It manufactures electricity meters, smart meters, gas meters, and water meters, and provides end-to-end Advanced Metering Infrastructure Service Provider (AMISP) solutions under the government's Revamped Distribution Sector Scheme (RDSS).

**Business Verticals:**
- **AMISP / Smart Metering (Core)** — Design, supply, installation, and maintenance of smart meters under 8-9 year concession contracts. 24 AMISP projects all achieved Operational Go-Live (OGL).
- **Manufacturing** — 18M+ meters/year capacity across integrated facilities.
- **Exports** — Renewed post-COVID push, targeting ₹500 Cr in 2-3 years.
- **Gas & Water Meters** — Adjacent growth verticals being scaled.
- **Software / Platform** — HES, MDMS, WFM — 250+ in software R&D, 225+ in device R&D.

**Key Milestones:** Crossed **1 crore smart meters installed** under RDSS during FY26 | Cumulative **over 2.6 crore meters deployed worldwide** | Manufacturing capacity exceeding **18 million meters annually**
