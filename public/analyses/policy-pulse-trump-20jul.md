### 2. 📅 Section 122 Tariff Expiry: 4 Days to Go — July 24, 2026

The 10-15% global Section 122 import surcharge expires by statute on **July 24, 2026** (just 4 days away). The 150-day limit under the Trade Act of 1974 cannot be unilaterally extended by the President — Congress must act. Key status:

- No extension bill has been introduced in Congress
- The June 11 Federal Circuit stay keeps the tariff in force pending appeal
- Three scenarios: (1) Congress extends, (2) replacement via Section 301/232, (3) **lapse with no replacement** — the most bullish scenario for retailers
- If it lapses, the 10% surcharge disappears overnight on **consumer electronics, apparel, footwear, home goods, furniture** from China, Vietnam, India, Bangladesh
- US retailers (WMT, TGT, COST, AMZN, HD, LOW) are direct beneficiaries of a tariff lapse
