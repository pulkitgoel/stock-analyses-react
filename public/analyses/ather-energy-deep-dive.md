## 2. Business Overview

Ather Energy is an Indian electric two-wheeler (E2W) manufacturer headquartered in Bengaluru, incorporated in 2013. The company went public in May 2025 at an issue price of ₹321, raising ₹2,981 Cr.

The company designs, develops, and manufactures:
- **Electric Scooters** — 450X (performance) and Rizta (family) variants
- **Battery Packs** — manufactured in-house (vertically integrated)
- **Charging Infrastructure** — Ather Grid (5,000+ charging points pan-India)
- **Software Ecosystem** — AtherStack Pro (93% attach rate in Q4 FY26)

### Key Differentiators

- **Vertically integrated** — only E2W company manufacturing battery packs in-house
- **Software-defined vehicle** — AtherStack Pro subscription creates recurring, high-margin revenue
- **4th largest E2W manufacturer** in India by sales volume (18.8% market share in Q3 FY26)
- **700+ Experience Centres** pan-India as of Mar'26
- **Non-vehicle revenue** (software, charging, spares, service) at 13% of total income — structurally growing

### Management

- **Tarun Mehta** — Co-founder, CEO & Executive Director
- **Swapnil Jain** — Co-founder
