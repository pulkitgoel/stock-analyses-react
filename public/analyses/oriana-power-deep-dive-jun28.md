## 2. Business Overview

Oriana Power operates across three integrated verticals:

**1. Solar EPC (99% of current revenue)**
- Design, engineering, procurement, and construction of solar power plants
- 835+ MWp delivered, 700+ MWp under execution
- ₹1,200 Cr Maithon floating solar project (world's largest) won in FY26
- CRISIL A-/Stable rating

**2. Battery Energy Storage (BESS) — Rapidly Scaling**
- 1,500+ MWh under execution, 3,000+ MWh in pipeline
- Commissioned Rajasthan's first group captive open access solar + BESS
- Delivered on-ground BESS capacity (few players have actually executed)
- Target: 40% of FY27 revenue, 60% of FY28 revenue

**3. Green Fuels — Future Growth Engine**
- Signed 10-year green ammonia PPA with SECI (fertilizer company offtaker)
- 60 KTPA green ammonia, project value ~₹3,000 Cr
- Pre-FEED done, FEED study underway (completion Q3 FY27)
- No revenue in FY27, ~10% contribution in FY28

## 8. Actis Deal (The Big Catalyst)

**What it is:** Strategic partnership with Actis (global infrastructure investor) for:
- Monetization of 238 MWp operational solar assets (H1 FY27)
- Joint development of a 1 GWp platform
- ₹4,000 Cr firm commitment by Actis

**Why it was delayed (FY26 → FY27):**
- BESS components were added to asset mix (deal size increased)
- Regulatory and platform restructuring took extra time
- **Management sees this as a POSITIVE** — bigger deal, better structure

**Timeline:**
- H1 FY27: 238 MWp monetization
- H2 FY27: 200 MWp monetization
- FY28: 400 MWp (200+200)
- FY29: 400 MWp

**Impact:** Capital recycling → debt reduction → balance sheet strengthening → better credit rating

## 11. Mainboard Migration — The Single Biggest Structural Catalyst

Oriana Power is listed on the **NSE SME Emerge** platform since its IPO in **August 2023**. Management has repeatedly confirmed that mainboard migration is a **top strategic priority**.

### What Management Said (Directly from the Transcript)

During the **June 10, 2025 Concall (FY25 H2)**, the following exchange took place:

> **Moderator (Amit Kumar):** *"Another interesting question — the company is targeting to migrate to the main board."*
>
> **Anirudh Saraswat (Whole-time Director):** *"Yes, sir definitely."*
>
> **Rupal Gupta (Managing Director & CEO):** *"So, that is where the second level of leadership is being part of Oriana and being added every month I would say so. It is a need, and we are heading towards it."*
>
> **Varun Prabhakar (General Counsel — newly appointed):** *"Subject to the compliance of the regulatory, then we will migrate to the main Board."*
>
> **Rupal Gupta:** *"So, that is where Varun Prabhakar and the team will play a major role for the direction."*

**Source:** [Oriana Power FY25 Investor Transcript (June 10, 2025), Section: Main Board Migration](https://orianapower.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Oriana_InvestorTranscript_10062025.pdf) — Official company filing to NSE under Regulation 30.

This is not coincidental — it's a deliberate, structured move toward migration readiness.

Oriana Power is building a genuinely differentiated renewable energy platform spanning generation (solar), storage (BESS), and consumption (green fuels). The business fundamentals (₹7,000 Cr order book, 122% 5-yr sales CAGR, CRISIL A- rating) are strong. The Actis deal in H1 FY27 is a significant catalyst that could trigger a re-rating.

**However**, the stock in a bear market (-48% from highs), FIIs have abandoned ship, and management credibility needs rebuilding. The stock is cheap for a reason.

**Ideal Scenario:** If Actis monetization happens on schedule (H1 FY27) AND management delivers 40-50% CAGR, the stock could re-rate from 12.8x to 20x P/E, implying 50%+ upside from current levels.

**Risk Scenario:** If Actis gets delayed further or commodity prices stay elevated, the stock could test its 52W low of ₹1,500 or break below.
