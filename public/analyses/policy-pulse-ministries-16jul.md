## 🏛️ Ministry & Regulatory Roundup

### 1. SEBI Buy-Back Amendment — Effective Aug 1, 2026

SEBI notified the **SEBI (Buy-Back of Securities) (Amendment) Regulations, 2026** on July 1, effective **August 1, 2026**. Key changes:
- **15% cap** on stock exchange buybacks (≤15% of paid-up capital + free reserves)
- Open market buybacks **reintroduced** from August 1
- Merchant banker requirement **made optional** for open market buybacks
- Companies prohibited from buybacks that breach minimum public shareholding (MPS) rules

**Impact:** Makes buybacks easier for smaller companies (no mandatory merchant banker) but caps large-scale buybacks on exchanges. Cash-rich companies with strong free reserves are best positioned.

### 2. SEBI LODR (Second Amendment) — July 10, 2026

SEBI amended LODR regulations to move transfer/transmission procedures from Schedule VII to SEBI-specified procedures. Effective immediately. This streamlines compliance but is broadly neutral for markets.

### 3. DAC Clears ₹52,000 Crore Defence Acquisitions (Jul 3, 2026)

The Defence Acquisition Council, chaired by Rajnath Singh, cleared capital acquisition proposals worth ~₹52,000 crore. Key projects:
- **Akash Tarang** anti-drone system
- **MPATGM** (Man Portable Anti-Tank Guided Missile)
- **V-SHORADS** (Very Short Range Air Defence System)
- **MRSAM** (Medium Range Surface to Air Missile)
- Kamikaze drones and anti-drone systems
- Pseudo-satellites

**Note:** PIB does not name specific companies. The below company mappings are **my inferences based on historical vendor relationships and past DAC contract awards.**

### 4. RBI — Status Quo (June MPC)

RBI held repo rate at **5.25%** (unchanged) with neutral stance. GDP projected at 6.6% for FY27. No new July announcements.
