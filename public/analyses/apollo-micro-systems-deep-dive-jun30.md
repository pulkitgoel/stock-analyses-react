## 2. Business Overview

Apollo Micro Systems Ltd (AMS) is a Hyderabad-based defence technology company specializing in **design, development, assembly, and testing of electronic and electro-mechanical solutions** for the defence sector. Founded in 1997 by Baddam Karunakar Reddy.

### Core Verticals

| Domain | Key Products |
|--------|-------------|
| **Missile Systems** | Sub-systems for BrahMos, Pinaka, Akash NG, QRSAM, Nag, Helina, Pralay, Agni |
| **Naval Systems** | Varunastra (torpedo signal processing), Multi-Influence Ground Mines (MIGM), Moored Mines, Limpet Mines, EHWT homing systems |
| **Underwater Weapons** | ALWT trials, torpedo decoys |
| **Drones & UAV** | DPIIT license awarded; loitering munitions, counter-drone systems |
| **Explosives** | Through subsidiary IDL Explosives (ADIPL) — HMX 50 MTPA |
| **Homeland Security** | Vehicle-mounted counter-drone systems, EW systems |

### Competitive Moat

- **Only company with highest participation in Indigenous Missile Programs of DRDO** — 60+ DcPP programs
- **700+ onboard technologies**, 150+ indigenous programs
- **License to manufacture complete weapons** — missiles, torpedoes, underwater mines, aerial bombs
- **Only approved production agency** for Multi-Influence Ground Mine (Vighana) — India now joins US, Russia, China, Italy in this tech
- **Zero R&D attrition** — 8% of revenue spent on R&D

> **"The only company in India with a proven track record of supplying from an individual system to complete platforms, including explosives."** — Management PPT
