## 🇮🇳 India's First Hydrogen Train Flagged Off — Jind-Sonipat Route

PM Modi flagged off India's first hydrogen-powered passenger train at Jind, Haryana on July 17. The 10-coach hydrogen fuel cell train carries ~2,600 passengers with a 3,200 HP propulsion system — reportedly the **world's largest capacity hydrogen train** in operation. Designed, engineered, and integrated in India.

**Key details:**
- Runs between Jind and Sonipat (Haryana), a non-electrified route
- Produces only water vapor as by-product — zero emissions
- Hydrogen refueling facility established at Jind
- Safety: multi-layer hydrogen leak, heat, flame, and smoke detectors
