## 2. Business Overview

Anant Raj Ltd, incorporated in 1985, is a Delhi-NCR based real estate developer with a rich legacy spanning over five decades. The company has successfully developed over 11.3 MSF of real estate across residential, commercial, IT parks, and hospitality sectors. In recent years, it has made a strategic pivot into **data centre and cloud services** via its wholly-owned subsidiary Anant Raj Cloud Pvt. Ltd. (ARCPL).

**Key Business Segments:**

- **Real Estate Development:** Residential townships, group housing (luxury segment in Sector 63A, Gurugram), commercial complexes, IT parks, and hospitality
- **Data Centre & Cloud Services:** 28 MW operational IT load (21 MW Manesar + 7 MW Panchkula); targeting 357 MW total by FY2032
- **Rental Income:** Commercial and hospitality assets generating steady annuity income

**Key Highlights:**
- Zero net debt company (5th consecutive quarter)
- Land bank of ~1,038 acres in NCR (fully paid)
- Sovereign cloud provider (Ashok Cloud) — MeitY empanelled
- Strategic partnership with Submer (Spain) for liquid-cooled AI data centres
- Investment of ₹20,000-₹25,000 Cr planned for DC expansion via MoU with Haryana government
