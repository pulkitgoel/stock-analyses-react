Purchases included Apple, Microsoft, Nvidia, Amazon, and Alphabet (each up to $250,000). The next day, Trump posted "THIS IS A GREAT TIME TO BUY!!!" on Truth Social before announcing the tariff pause, sending the S&P 500 up ~9.5% in a single session — one of the largest single-day gains in history.

## 🇺🇸 Trump Accounts Program Launch

On July 4, 2026 (America's 250th anniversary), the U.S. Treasury officially launched **Trump Accounts** — a savings/investment platform giving eligible children (born 2025-2028) a $1,000 government seed contribution. The program went live with BNY as the financial agent and Robinhood as the sole initial trustee and broker. 6 million accounts have already been opened.

The program channels massive capital flows into **State Street's SPTI ETF** ($157B AUM, 0.02% ER). Reuters reported this tests the independence of Big 3 asset managers (BlackRock, Vanguard, State Street) who face potential Trump administration influence over proxy voting.
