### 2. 💊 Trump Announces Generic Drug Tariff Timeline

In a Truth Social post on July 22, Trump announced a phased tariff timeline for generic pharmaceutical imports:
- **Aug 1, 2026 – Aug 1, 2028:** Zero tariffs (2-year grace period)
- **Aug 1, 2028:** 100% tariff
- **Aug 1, 2029:** 200% tariff

The stated goal is to reshore generic pharmaceutical production. Indian companies supply ~40% of U.S. generic drugs ($10B in exports). While the 2-year window provides some buffer, the long-term threat is material for US-facing generic players.
