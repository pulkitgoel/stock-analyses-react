## 🇮🇳 PM Modi in Australia: Uranium Deal, Defence Declaration, A$500M Investment

PM Modi visited Australia (Jul 8-10, 2026) for the **Third Australia-India Annual Summit** in Melbourne. **Major outcomes with stock-level impact:**

1. **🇦🇺 Uranium Supply Deal** — Australia and India signed an agreement to export Australian uranium to India for use in nuclear energy, supporting India's 100 GW nuclear target by 2047.
2. **🇦🇺 A$500M Investment** — AustralianSuper (Australia's largest pension fund) announced an additional A$500M ($347M) investment in India's **National Investment and Infrastructure Fund (NIIF)**.
3. **🇦🇺 New Defence Declaration** — India-Australia defence declaration to deepen Indo-Pacific cooperation.
4. **🇦🇺 Clean Energy Cooperation** — Australia's technology, capital, and resources to accelerate India's 500 GW renewable target by 2030. Low-carbon aluminium projects signalled.
5. **🇦🇺 CECA Push** — Both leaders called for early conclusion of the Comprehensive Economic Cooperation Agreement.
6. **🇦🇺 Education** — Approval for 8 Australian branch campuses in India; Flinders University is the latest.
7. **🇦🇺 Sports Collaboration** — Roadmap for sports cooperation ahead of Brisbane 2032 Olympics and Ahmedabad 2030 Commonwealth Games.
8. **🇦🇺 Business Delegation** — Business Council of Australia leading senior delegation to India in December 2026. New investment roundtable in Mumbai via Austrade.
