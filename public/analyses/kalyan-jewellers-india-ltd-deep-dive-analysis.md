### 2. Business Overview

**Company:** Kalyan Jewellers India Ltd. is one of India's largest organised jewellery retailers, founded in 1993 by T.S. Kalyanaraman. The company operates a hyperlocal retail model with 524+ showrooms across India, the Middle East, and the UK.

**Brand Portfolio:**
- **Kalyan Jewellers** — Mainline brand (354 stores in India, Middle East)
- **Candere** — Omnichannel lifestyle jewellery (129 outlets, 70%+ studded mix)
- **Regional brand** — New brand launching in South India (under development)

**Business Model:**
- Primarily **FOCO (Franchise Owned Company Operated)** — capital-light expansion
- Asset-light: franchisees fund real estate, Kalyan funds inventory
- Revenue: Gold jewellery (~70%), studded jewellery (~25%), other (coins, silver)

**Key Strengths:**
- Pan-India presence with deep penetration in Tier-2/3 cities
- Gold savings scheme drives sticky customer base
- Aggressive store expansion (150 new stores planned FY27)
- Candere turning profitable — digital growth engine
- Strong same-store sales growth (20-30% over last 2-3 years)
