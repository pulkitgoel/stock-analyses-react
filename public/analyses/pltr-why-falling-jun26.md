## Why Is PLTR Falling?

### 1️⃣ Multiple Compression — The  Reason
Entered 2026 at P/E ~155x and P/S ~62x. After doubling in 2025, the stock had zero margin for error. Market rotated from "growth at any price" to value. Even after -32% YTD, forward P/E is still ~84x.

### 2️⃣ "Buy the Rumor, Sell the News"
Q1 was a blowout ($1.633B, +85% YoY, largest-ever guidance raise) yet stock fell earnings day ($144 → $133). Everything was already priced in.
