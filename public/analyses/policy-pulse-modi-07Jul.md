# Policy Pulse — Modi — 07 Jul 2026

**Date:** July 7, 2026

## Overview

Prime Minister Modi's three-nation visit (Indonesia → Australia → New Zealand) produced significant defense and trade outcomes on Day 1 in Jakarta.
