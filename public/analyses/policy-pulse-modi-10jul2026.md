## 🇮🇳 India's Semiconductor Mission: From Vision to Reality — $18B, 10 Projects, Commercial Production Begins

PM Modi's Semicon India programme is transitioning from vision to execution. **Key milestones achieved as of mid-2026:**

- **10 semiconductor projects** attracting over **$18 billion** (₹1.5 lakh crore) in investment
- **Commercial chip packaging has begun at Sanand** (Gujarat) — first commercial output from India's semiconductor ecosystem
- **5 additional projects cleared in 2025**, following the first plant approved in 2023
- **Gujarat to build SAMARTH hub** at IIT Gandhinagar with $19.96M for semiconductor R&D and talent development
- Target: **Top 6 semiconductor manufacturing nations** with five plants by end-2026

**Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw** has set the target of India becoming a top 6 semiconductor producer. The India Semiconductor Mission (ISM) launched with ₹76,000 Cr incentive package is driving this transformation.

## 🇮🇳 India's Defence Production and Exports Hit Record Highs

Ministry of Defence data shows **unprecedented momentum in India's defence industrial base**:

- **Annual defence production:** ₹1.78 lakh crore (FY26 — all-time high)
- **Defence exports:** Record ₹38,424 Cr ($4.6B) — supplying **80+ countries**
- **Target:** ₹50,000 Cr ($6B) in defence exports by 2029-30
- **₹52,000 Cr ($5B+) defence package approved** via DAC including Akash Tarang, MPATGM, MRSAM, V-SHORADS, kamikaze drones, HAPS
- **Defence Secretary confirmed:** Ministry plans to sign **₹2 lakh crore in contracts in FY26**
- India's defence procurement push accelerated post-Operation Sindoor

Key systems approved by DAC:
- **Akash Tarang** — anti-drone system
- **MPATGM** — anti-tank missiles
- **MRSAM & V-SHORADS** — air defence systems
- **Jet-powered kamikaze drones** for the Army
- **Multi Influence Ground Mines** and **Naval Shipborne UAVs** for Navy
- **High-Altitude Pseudo Satellites (HAPS)** for IAF surveillance
