## 🇺🇸 Trump-US Policy Watch: Canada 50% Tariffs (Jul 20)

President Trump signed **three proclamations** on Jul 20 imposing **50% tariffs** on Canadian goods — the first use of Section 338 of the Tariff Act of 1930 in nearly a century. The tariffs cover:
- **Motor vehicles**: Canada's quotas penalize US auto imports vs other countries
- **Alcoholic beverages**: Canadian provinces halted US alcohol purchases (81% drop in imports)
- **Dairy**: Canada's TRQs on US cheese are more restrictive than EU cheese quotas

**Effective date:** 30 days after signing (mid-August 2026)
**Magnitude:** ~$20B of Canadian imports affected (5.2% of total US imports from Canada)
**Excluded:** Energy, potash, critical minerals, goods already under Section 232
