---

## 1. ISM 2.0: ₹1.25 Lakh Crore Outlay (16x ISM 1.0)

**What happened:** The EFC under the Finance Ministry approved ₹1.25 lakh crore for ISM 2.0, up from ₹76,000 crore in ISM 1.0. The proposal now goes to Union Cabinet for final approval. The mission aims for 75% domestic chip demand fulfilment by 2030.

**Key Details:**
- **Scope expanded** to cover entire semiconductor value chain: fabrication, equipment, materials, indigenous designs, specialty chemicals, industrial gases
- **New DLI (Design-Linked Incentive) scheme** — foreign companies can partner with Indian firms for R&D done in India
- **MSMEs and ancillary suppliers** brought under incentive framework
- 12 projects already approved with ₹1.64 lakh crore investment pipeline
- Targets complete ecosystem: silicon fabs, compound semiconductors, ATMP, OSAT, chip design

## 2. CG Semi Commercial Production Starts July 4

**What happened:** PM Modi will formally inaugurate CG Semi's commercial production at Sanand, Gujarat on July 4. The facility has already dispatched its first commercial chip consignments to Kuala Lumpur for Renesas customers — marking India's first commercial semiconductor packaging exports.

**Key Details:**
- **Joint venture:** CG Power (Murugappa Group) + Renesas Electronics (Japan) + Stars Microelectronics (Thailand)
- **Investment:** ₹7,600 crore ($870M) over 5 years
- **Capacity:** G1 facility — 0.5 million units/day peak; G2 under construction adding 14.5 million chips/day
- **Third operational unit** under India Semiconductor Mission (after Micron ATMP Sanand, Kaynes Semicon OSAT)
- CG Power also evaluating investments in **semiconductor design startups, acquisitions, and AI-focused technologies** through subsidiary Axiro
