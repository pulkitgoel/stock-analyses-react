## 🏭 Cabinet Approves ₹2.19 Lakh Crore Mega-Package

The Union Cabinet chaired by PM Modi approved a **massive ₹2.19 lakh crore package** on July 15, 2026, spanning semiconductors, mobile manufacturing, and infrastructure. Here's what was announced:

### 1. Semicon 2.0 — ₹1.27 Lakh Crore ($13.3B)

The **India Semiconductor Mission 2.0** expands the government's chip program with fresh capital of ₹1.27 lakh crore. This builds on Semicon 1.0 and aims to move India from hopeful participant to serious player in the global semiconductor supply chain. Five semiconductor plants expected operational by end of 2026, including the CG Semi OSAT facility in Sanand (joint venture: CG Power + Renesas + Stars Microelectronics).

### 2. Mobile Phone Manufacturing Scheme (MPMS) — ₹62,500 Crore

The new **Mobile Phone Manufacturing Scheme** replaces the expiring PLI-LSEM (which ended March 31, 2026). Running FY2026-27 to FY2030-31, it targets:
- Cumulative production of **₹39 lakh crore**
- **₹15 lakh crore in exports**
- **60,000 direct jobs**
- Move from assembly to Indian brand design & IP ownership
- Apple (via Foxconn, Tata Electronics, Pegatron) remains the largest exporter — India exported smartphones worth ₹2.62L cr in 2025

### 3. Infrastructure Projects

Six-lane elevated corridor in Varanasi and other railway/infra upgrades were also approved as part of the package.
