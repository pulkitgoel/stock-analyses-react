## Section 122 Tariff Expiry — 7 Days to Go (Jul 24)

The 10% Section 122 global import surcharge, imposed on Feb 24 after SCOTUS struck down IEEPA tariffs, expires by statute on **July 24, 2026** — just 7 days away. The President cannot extend it unilaterally; only Congress can act, and no extension bill has been introduced.

**Replacement mechanism:** USTR's July 20 deadline for Section 301 findings is just 3 days away. The proposal: **12.5% tariffs on 46 countries** including China, Vietnam, India, Thailand, Japan, and South Korea. Section 301 has no rate cap and no time limit.

**Key dates this week:**
- **Jul 20:** USTR Section 301 completion deadline (proposed 12.5% on 46 countries)
- **Jul 24:** Section 122 expires at 12:01 AM EDT — 10% surcharge vanishes unless replaced
